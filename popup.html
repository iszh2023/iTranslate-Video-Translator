Instructions
Scroll to the bottom, then press OK.
1) Open a video page (YouTube works best).
2) Turn on captions/subtitles (CC button).
3) If you want another language on YouTube:
Gear ⚙️ → Subtitles/CC → Auto-translate → pick a language.
4) Click this extension icon → press Read.
5) Press Stop to stop talking.
Note: This extension does not use external translation APIs. It reads whatever captions are shown on-screen (including auto-translated captions).
If nothing happens: captions might be off, the site might not show captions, or you may be on a restricted page (like chrome:// pages).
